If any beneficiary under this Last Will and Testament seeks to obtain, in any proceeding and in any Court, an adjudication that this Last Will and Testament or any of its provisions is void, or seeks otherwise to void, nullify, or set aside this Last Will and Testament or any of its provisions, then the right of that person to take any interest given to him or her by this Last Will and Testament shall be determined as it would have been determined had such person predeceased the execution of this Last Will and Testament without issue.
